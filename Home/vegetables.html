Green Valley Farms

Green Valley Farms is commitmented to organic farming practices and sustainability. Located in the lush hills of Kandy, Sri Lanka, Green Valley Farms is nestled amidst picturesque tea plantations and rolling hills.

Urban Harvest Farms

Urban Harvest Farm bringings locally grown vegetables to the tables of Colombo. This centrally located farm ensures easy access to fresh produce for supermarkets throughout the city.

Coastal Greens Farms

Coastal Greens Farm harness the natural resources of the coast to cultivate a wide variety of fresh vegetables where quality is their top priority. Located along the scenic coastline of Panadura.
